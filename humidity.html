Summary: Lattitude vs Humidity

Humidity is scattered everywhere as it is based on the amount of water in the atmosphere. Here in Kansas we have days with low humidity and days with high humidity (heat indeex during the summer is based on the humidity in the air). In Miami they also have high and low humidity days just like you would see if you lived at the equator. This is not a good correlation.
